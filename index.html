" And the responsibility of making the world a better place to live and making the nation proud lies on the shoulders of young India." We, the family of national service scheme (NSS) welcomes you to our page. This site has been dedicated to keep you all updated with the time to time activities of NSS and at the same time encourage you all to be the part of change that NSSJCBUST , Faridabad is trying to make through the country. We are grateful to **Dr Pradeep Dhimri sir our dearest co-ordinator, Dr Bindu Mangla mam , Umesh Kumar sir and** Nitin sir for their guidance and support to build this site and at the same time for their time to time encouragement that helped us reach here . We , the NSS family is out there to help India fight with mental and emotional issues along with fighting issues such as poverty , girl child abuse and everything else that acts as an obstacle to our motherland's prosperity . So here we are , taking you all on a journey full of NSS achievements , emotional and proud moments . Do join our journey and be a member of our tears and happiness ♥️
